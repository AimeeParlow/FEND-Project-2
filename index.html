About Me

FEND Scholarship...

I am a freelance translator working through internet for clients all over the world. I live in France that is not my home country and I am a foreigner now and forever. Because of that, I need to find something to survive, so working on internet is the perfect way. Now I am planning to provide web support plus localization service in the future. And luckily, I found Google Front-End Nanodegree Scholarship challenge and I have been selected to have it!

It was about 20 years ago that I got my first PC. At that time, there were no blog services, nor SNS. To share my life abroad to my family, I tried creating my own simple homepages, and I loved to do it. Although I was just at the entrance of web development world, I didn’t really go in. This Udacity's program is a perfect opportunity to give me courage to open the door and discover this world!
